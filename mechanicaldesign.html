Mechanical Design & Development

As a part of our mechanical engineering, we offer a vast range of engineering solutions to meet our diverse needs of our customers.

The listed below are all of the services we offer

1. PLM Implementation and customization services
Today's products are getting more and more complicated that makes the companies develop strategic steps before they can launch relevant products. The product life cycle begins with the introduction phase and cascades into conceptualization, design, testing, manufacturing stages. To remain competitive, the businesses must create indicators (technical, environmental, financial, etc.), determine the characteristics and capabilities of the product, and quantify the market opportunities.

2. 3D modelling and 3D mechanical drafting
3D modeling helps develop a mathematical, wireframe representation of any three-dimensional object. 3D model visualization helps you effectively communicate ideas and clarify project goals - about interference, tolerance, and aesthetics.

3. Reverse engineering
RE services can help you discover the technological details of a device, object, or system through an analysis of its structure, function, and operation. This process can provide valuable inputs to help create a superior and improvised design.

4. Plastic Injection and blow Moulding
Plastics are an integral part of many of the products we use in our daily lives. When you outsource mold design, mold flow analysis and plastic production to us, you gain access to expertise that can help you meet cost challenges and maintain the high-quality of the injection plastic part. Flat world Solutions can help you with prototyping, product design, mold tool design, and analysis as well as manufacturing.

5. Finite element analysis
Structural analysis is a valuable step in manufacturing set-ups where material, weight, cost, etc., must be refined after optimization while determining the safety factor. In addition, product designers can use it to analyze which design variables affect product performance and its impact.

6. Manufacturing and process planning
Our services can help you plan your manufacturing process so that you can reduce your time-to-market, effectively utilize manpower, optimize the use of materials, and achieve quality standards. This planning service includes process planning, master scheduling, requirements planning, and capacity planning (manufacturing control, shop floor control, and inventory control).

7. Material handling system design
Material handling refers to the movement, storage, protection and control of materials and products during manufacturing, storage, distribution, consumption, and disposal. It covers a wide range of manual, semi-automatic and automated equipment, logistics, making the operation of the supply chain possible.

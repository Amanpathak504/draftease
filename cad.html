What is CAD Conversion & Migration

Conversion focuses on conversion of your construction plans, scans, hand-drawn sketches, hard copies, structural blueprints and more into easy to use digitized/CAD format.
CAD migration is performed to convert/migrate data from various distinct platforms such as Dassault Systems, CATIA V4 / V5, AutoCAD / Inventor suites, Pro/E Wildfire etc., and then unify under one common platform.

Services offered under CAD Conversion

1. CAD digitization

Our team has the required skills and expertise to provide you with accurate digitized versions of your plans, sketches, pdf's etc.

2. CAD Conversions
a. Paper to CAD Conversion
b. PDF to CAD Conversion
c. 2D to 3D Conversion
d. AutoCAD Conversion
e. 2D drafting of mechanical parts and assemblies
f. BOM (Bill of Material)
g. 2D & 3D drawings from sketches

Services offered under CAD Migration

Migration to Solid works: Migrate all your existing drawings into solid works format (sldprt) might be an individual part or the entire assembly.

Migration to newer versions (CATIA V4-->V5): we can help you with conversion of existing drawings into CAD formats by following all your norms with the latest standards.
